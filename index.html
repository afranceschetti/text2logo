Welcome to Text 2 Logo

Text2Logo core

This site is designed for those who are starting a new business craving for a cool logo, but hasn't great graphics capabilities, and little money to pay for a graphic

What you can do

Choose up to 5 different text boxes, single letter, symbol or mini sentences, position it and customize it, try different fonts, colours and layouts.

At any time, you can save a picture, or save your work online, creating your own permalink

Teamwork

Text2Logo is designed for teamwork: simply prepare a first version of the logo, create the link and send it to your partner, which can continue your work, and send you back ...

Social Sharing

Who better than your friends can give you advice? Share the link on Facebook, or Google+ or whatever you want, they will appreciate your work directly from the web
